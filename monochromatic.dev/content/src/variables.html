Overriding Variables

There are several methods of overriding variables.

In the order of specificity (the more specific one overrides the less specific one):

content/**/.toml

Applies to all files in folder, recursively.

content/.extra.toml

Applies to all files in folder, recursively.

content/Article_Name.toml

Applies to Article_Name.html

content/Article_Name.extra.toml

x-variable in file.
